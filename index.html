Our Mission

To locate, stabilise and transport people in distress from hard-to-access areas to appropriate care facilities, using a blend of community networks, trained responders, and technology — while preserving privacy and dignity.

What We Do

Search & Rescue Operations

Deploy trained teams with aerial and ground assets to reach and extract people from challenging environments.

Community Emergency Hubs

Local hubs offering first-response equipment, volunteers, and secure access to the Foundation’s platform.

Personal Beacons & App

Privacy-first personal beacons and mobile app for instant alerts, location sharing and medical info access.

Training & Capacity Building

Courses for volunteers and professionals: triage, rope rescue, air evacuation basics and incident command.

How It Works

  1. Alert — Activate the app or beacon to send an encrypted SOS to our coordination center.
  2. Locate — Real-time tracking and nearest hub/asset identification.
  3. Respond — Dispatch the right asset: volunteer team, ambulance, or helicopter.
  4. Transfer — Transport to the chosen medical facility and assist with claims & follow-up.
